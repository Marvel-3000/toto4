WELCOME TO VIT

explore the culture of vit

VIT Pune is an engineering college located in Pune, Maharashtra, India. The college was established in 1983 and has since then grown to become one of the most reputed engineering colleges in India. VIT Pune offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various disciplines such as Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Information Technology. The college is affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). VIT Pune has state-of-the-art facilities, highly qualified faculty members, and a vibrant campus life that offers a holistic learning experience to its students.
